Purpose

I'm a 16 year old high school student with a passion for digital initiatives including social media, computer programming, and community outreach.

I've built apps for Windows Phone, BlackBerry 10, Android, and iOS. My very latest project has been WS Companion, an app for my high school.

When not programming, I work with my school district to promote responsible social media use. I've spent time in my local elementary schools to achieve this goal. In one of those schools, I work with a group of students, teaching them web design and development.

As a National Youth Ambassador for The Next Big Thing Foundation, I work to build the next generation of business leaders and innovators. I also spend time with HootSuite, to further my engineering skills.
